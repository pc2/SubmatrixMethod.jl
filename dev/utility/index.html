SubmatrixMethod.spectral_norm — Method

Computes the spectral norm of the input matrix:

\[\|A\|_{2}=\sqrt{\lambda_{\max }\left(A^{H} A\right)},\]

i.e. the square root of the maximal eigenvalue of A'*A.
